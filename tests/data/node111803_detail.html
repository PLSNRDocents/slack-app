    Activity Description

    Join Peter Fletcher to help our visitors discover and understand some amazing sea creatures and plants. Be prepared for low water, great tide pools, and lots of fun exploring the pools.    Water-proof boots are useful but not required, you can try to keep your feet dry or have even more fun by wearing closed-toed shoes that you are willing to get wet!   Low tide is at 4:49 pm.
